what_are_the_roles_in_credit360_resp

In Credit360 users are set up with different levels of access and responsibilities called Roles. This is for security and system integrity control.
Power Users are being established across all zones to manage user access and assign these roles.
- *Larger facilities* will have Power Users to manage the system;
- *Small facilities* may have Power Users at the Regional level;

All incidents will follow a pre-established workflow to allow for data entry and final approval and closure of the investigation.
