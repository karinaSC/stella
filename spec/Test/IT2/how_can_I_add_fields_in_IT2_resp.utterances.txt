To add fields launch *Add or remove fields* window.
The list on the left shows fields that are available to be added. Simply highlight the desired field and click on *add* to add the field to the Workbench.
Fields that are highlighted in blue could be added to the Workbench multiple time whereas the one is black could only be added once.
Click on selected field and press the *Properties* button on the right hand corner.
Change the field display name by editing the name shown in the header section.
Define the amount CCY from the *Conversions* tab.
You can also configure the rate set and rate dates used."
